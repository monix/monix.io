Resources:

Abstract #

Concurrency bugs leave you restless? Monix Catnap provides a ton of utilities that will let your worries take a good nap!

Monix Catnap and Execution are rather unknown modules of Monix which have a lot of useful features like Local, ConcurrentQueue and ConcurrentChannel for everyone to use regardless of their effect of choice.

The talk will cover some of the most interesting ones and show how they could be of use in your projects.
